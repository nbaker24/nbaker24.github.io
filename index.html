ABOUT US PRODUCTS SERVICES CUSTOM QUOTE Systems Pack, Inc.
Need affordable packaging and shipping supplies? Systems Pack, Inc. offers diverse packaging solutions to solve your specific packaging needs. From corrugated boxes to polyethylene bags, strapping to shrink films, precision plywood pallets to expendable pallets; we provide you with innovative, less costly methods of getting your product to market safely and efficiently. Our plant provides a complete range of packaging services to custom design a system that works for you. Our staff of professionals can assist you in package and unit design, from material selection through implementation.

Systems Pack can provide both standard and custom packaging products, with the industry's most complete assortment including popular categories such corrugated boxes, corrugated inserts and corrugated containers, shipping supplies, packing supplies, stretch wrap, and a full range of custom products. We also offer wooden pallets, crates, shipping boxes, wraps, cushioning, cartons, paperboard sheets, partitions & pads.

Systems Pack is also a distributor of packaging machinery, and can assist you with manual & automatic tapers, strapping, stretch & shrink wrapping & bag sealing machinery. We can also work with you to provide assistance with package design, unit design & material selection, and our converting services include flexible packaging products, tapes & strapping.
Systems Pack, Inc. is a Lantech Corporation sales and service representative for Ohio. Lantech is the leader in secondary packaging solutions, building stretch wrappers, case erectors , shrink wrap machines, palletizers, and palletload conveyors to unmatched quality standards.
