About Us
Systems Pack, Inc. was founded in 1973 and has built on its reputation on innovative technology and diversification, quality products and cost effective solutions to grow from a one room shop to a warehouse. When quality and dependability count, let Systems Pack, Inc. be your comprehensive packaging source.

Packaging products include wooden pallets, boxes, crates, corrugated boxes, wraps, cushioning, cartons, paperboard sheets and partitions & pads amoung others. We are a distributor of packaging machinery including manual & automatic carton sealing machines (known as a 'taper'), strapping, stretch & shrink wrapping and bag sealing machinery. We are also distributors of bags, shipping room supplies, hot melt adhesives and tapes. Our converting services include flexible packaging products, multiple tapes & strapping.

We can assist your business with package, unit design & material selection. We offer packaging products for various markets, which include, food, pharmaceutical & industrial. Our packaging products including fiberboard containers, inner wraps, desiccants & poly bags, all of which meet Military Specifications.
