About Us
For years, Systems Pack, Inc. has built its reputation on innovative technology and diversification, quality products and cost effective solutions. When quality and dependability count, let Systems Pack, Inc. be your comprehensive packaging source.

We are a custom manufacturer of packaging products. Packaging products include wooden pallets, lumber, boxes, crates, corrugated boxes, wraps, cushioning, cartons, paperboard sheets, partitions & pads. Distributor of packaging machinery including manual & automatic tapers, strapping, stretch & shrink wrapping & bag sealing machinery. Distributor of bags, shipping room supplies & hot melt adhesives & tapes. Converting services include flexible packaging products, tapes & strapping. Capabilities include assistance with package, unit design & material selection. Markets include food, pharmaceutical & industrial. Packaging products including fiberboard containers, inner wraps, desiccants & poly bags meet Military Spec.
