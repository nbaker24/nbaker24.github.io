Impulse Sealers
Adjustable timer on these impulse sealers allows perfect seals for a wide variety of poly thicknesses.
  • To create a seal, place bag or tubing between seal bars, press down and release.
  • No warm up needed.
  • For use with polyethylene and polypropylene.
