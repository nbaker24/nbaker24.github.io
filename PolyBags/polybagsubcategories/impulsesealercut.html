Impulse Sealers with Cutter
This unit will seal and clean cut poly tubing in one step.
  • To create a seal, place bag or tubing between seal bars and press down.
  • Use sliding cutter to take off excess tubing.
  • Use with polyethylene and polypropylene bags and tubing.
  • Service kits containing 2 heating elements, 2 PTFE glass cloth tape strips and 1 cutter are available for each sealer.
