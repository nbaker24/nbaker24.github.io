Kapton® Discs
Made from polyimide film with a silicone adhesive.
  • Recommended for masking small areas on circuit boards.
  • Discs are on a liner which is wound on a 3" core.
  • Silicone adhesive will not leave any residue.
  • Withstands temperatures of up to 500° F.
  • Applications include: Masking circuit boards during wave soldering, powder coating, and automotive and transformer manufacturing.
  • 1 Roll per case.
