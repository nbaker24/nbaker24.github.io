Kraft Central® 235 Reinforced Tape
Recommended for boxes up to 25 lbs.
Designed for fast, permanent adhesion and superior strength. Excellent flexibility for sealing carton corners, edges and irregular surfaces.
  • Extra strong fiberglass yarns are strategically bonded between sheets of paper to provide an extra margin of strength.
  • Feature patented starch based adhesive.
  • Water activated tape is not affected by extreme hot or cold temperatures.
  • Environmentally friendly; yarn can be removed during repulping to allow for easy recycling.
