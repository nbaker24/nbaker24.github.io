3M™ Stretchable Tape
High-performance transparent tape designed to contain pallets without crushing cartons.
  • Stretches up to 700%.
  • Un-stretched tape sticks on contact to corrugated with just enough strength to hold itself in place for an easy start to wrapping.
  • Won't tear labels.
  • Loses tack when stretched.
  • Reduces waste created by stretch film and netting by 95%.
  • Clear LDPE backing with synthetic rubber resin adhesive.
